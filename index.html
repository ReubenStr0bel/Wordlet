Wordlet

Guess the emoji combination before you run out of turns.

How to Play

In Wordlet, you are trying to guess a random combination of 5 emojis. You have 6 turns in which to guess the combination, and each time receive information about which guesses are correct.

A grey background indicates that this emoji is not part of the correct sequence. A yellow background indicates that the emoji is in the sequence, but not in that square. Finally, a green background indicates that you've placed the correct emoji in the correct square.

Click on to delete an emoji if you want to guess a different one. Then click on to submit your guess once you have guessed 5 emojis.

To start a game, click on the 'Play Game' button to navigate to the game page, and then click the 'New Game' button to select the emoji theme you want to use and start a new game. After each game you can clikc the new game button again to start another game and choose a different emoji set.
